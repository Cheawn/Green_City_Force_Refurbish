Welcome to Green City Force!

Green City Force is an AmeriCorps program that engages young adults from low income communities in national service related to the environment.

In doing so, we prepare them for sustainable careers, while reducing greenhouse gas emissions and building the green city we want to see.

Serve your community. Break the cycle of poverty. Learn more about joining our corps.

Get Involved with Green City Force

There are many ways to get involved with Green City Force! If you are interested in becoming a Green City Force AmeriCorps Member, please visit the Clean Energy Corps page and the subsequent pages. Our Corps Members are job-ready, skilled, team players, and make excellent employees.
